Aberdare Self Discovery walk

Sunset and Sunrise safari"Amboseli is one of the best spots in the world to watch elephant with the backdrop of Africa’s highest mountain, Mount Kilimanjaro. Writers have described Amboseli National Park as a home for the Gods’. The park also provides the best chance of spotting the elusive black rhino. Lion and cheetah are common as well as Maasai giraffe, eland, Coke's heartebeest, waterbuck, impala and gazelle.
