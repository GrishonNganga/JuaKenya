Who We Are

JuaKenya has long and prestigious history of 20 years of experience specializing in a range of high quality corporate travel and related services. We were established in February 1976 and was accredited as IATA Agent in April 1977. It is not a subsidiary of a parent company nor has it been merged or acquired by another company. Our Head Office is situated on Moi Avenue in the City Centre. We occupy two floors of the company-owned building.
