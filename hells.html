Hell's fitness Bootcamp

Location: Lake Naivasha

Hells gate national park is a 68 sq km located some 120km north west of Nairobi and on the floor of the Great Rift Valley. Hells gate Hiking. It is also known as the Lion King Movie Park or the Angelina Jolie acted movie Tomb Raiders -Cradle of mankind.

Amount: KES 8,500

More Details

1. - 7:30am: Hells gate National Park day trip starts after breakfast with an early pick up by our driver guide.
2. - 8:45am: Stop over at the Great Rift Valley view point on the eastern escarpment
to see Mt Longonot, Mt Suswa, and the expansive floor of the rift valley.
3. - 10:00am: Arrival at the hells gate national park bike riding (optional) and walking safari.
Your guide will assist you to hire a good bike for the hells gate bike ride. Hells gate guided walking
safari start immediately from the gate in the company of an experienced guide. (bike ride is optional)
4. - 1:00pm: Enjoy a picnic lunch in the hells gate national park picnic site with the view of the park and
as you watch the animals walking around.
5. - 2:00pm: You can choose to go for a further walking safari in hells gate national park or visit
the lake Naivasha to enjoy an optional boat ride and see hippos, birds, and the best views of the mountains
beyond. Optional visit to Mvuke spa swimming pool to swim in naturally heated and biggest swimming pool in Africa.
