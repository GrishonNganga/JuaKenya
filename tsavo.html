Tsavo Big 5 Drive

Location: Voi Taita Taveta

Tsavo is made up of two separate parks, Tsavo East National Park and Tsavo West National Park. Located in Coast Province. Tsavo East Features is generally flat, with dry plains across which the Galana River flows. Other features include the Yatta Plateau and Lugard Falls. Tsavo West Features is more mountainous and wetter with swamps, Lake Jipe and the Mzima Springs.
